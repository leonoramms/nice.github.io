Experiments and Research

Danish groups currently participate in the following experiments at CERN. Follow the links for more information about the experiments and the international collaborations.

CERN Experiments with dnaish participation:

  • ALICE: Physics of the Quark Gluon Plasma and the strong interaction. https://alice-collaboration.web.cern.ch
  • ATLAS: Test of the standard model (SM), Higgs, and searches for physics beyond the SM. https://atlas.cern
  • ISOLDE: Reactions with beams far from stability. https://isolde.web.cern.ch
  • ALPHA: Formation and properties of antihydrogen. https://alpha.web.cern.ch
  • CAST: Axion search. https://home.cern/science/experiments/cast
  • NA63: Strong EM fields. https://home.cern/science/experiments/na63
