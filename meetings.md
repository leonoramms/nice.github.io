## Meetings Overview

NICE holds regular coordination meetings to align activities, share progress, and discuss
strategic priorities across member institutions. All members and affiliated researchers are
encouraged to participate.

---

## Meeting Types

### Steering Committee Meetings
Held twice per year (spring and autumn). Attendance by institutional representatives is expected.
Agenda and minutes are circulated to all NICE members.

### Scientific Working Group Meetings
Working groups on specific technical topics (e.g., silicon sensors, gaseous detectors, DAQ)
meet on an ad-hoc basis, typically 3–4 times per year. Meetings are open to all NICE affiliates.

### Annual NICE Symposium
A full-day annual symposium bringing together all NICE members, affiliated researchers, and
invited guests from CERN and partner institutions. Includes scientific talks, poster sessions,
and a networking event.
